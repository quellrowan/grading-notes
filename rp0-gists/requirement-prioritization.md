# Requirement Prioritization Topic — RP-0 Gist

## Quick Read
Solid, valid topic with clear relevance to software projects and requirements engineering. The main issue is not quality so much as distinctiveness and specificity.

## Topic
Requirement prioritization in software projects.

## Why It Works
- Clearly within the course domain.
- The student understands the practical importance of prioritization.
- Includes a plausible curiosity around how decisions are actually made and what factors are over- or undervalued.
- Easy to connect to stakeholder conflict and project tradeoffs.

## Risks / Watchouts
- More generic than some of the stronger proposals.
- Could remain too broad unless narrowed to a context, method, or decision problem.
- Needs a more distinctive research angle than “prioritization is important.”

## Coaching Direction
Encourage the student to narrow by asking one sharper question, for example:
- compare prioritization methods
- study prioritization under stakeholder conflict
- examine whether teams overweight business value, urgency, or ease of implementation
