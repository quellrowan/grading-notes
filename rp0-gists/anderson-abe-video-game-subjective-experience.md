### Stronger Instructor Comment
This is a workable topic area, but right now it feels more like the beginning of a curiosity than a developed pitch. The core idea—how requirements engineering can deal with subjective goals like fun, immersion, or challenge—is absolutely interesting, but the proposal needs a more specific angle to become compelling. ### Promising Directions to Suggest
- Pick one subjective concept only: fun, immersion, challenge, or reward.
- Focus on measurable proxies for player experience.
- Explore how telemetry could be used to validate experience-related requirements.
- Compare vague experience goals against more operationalized design targets.
